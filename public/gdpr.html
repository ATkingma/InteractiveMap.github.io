GDPR Compliance

General Data Protection Regulation Compliance

Your Data Rights Under GDPR

As a user of Arena Breakout Maps, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to be informed: We provide clear information about how we collect and use your data
  • Right of access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing: You can request limitation of how we process your data
  • Right to data portability: You can request transfer of your data to another service
  • Right to object: You can object to our processing of your personal data

Data We Process

We process the following categories of data:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: Map interactions, marker placements, preferences
  • Cookie Data: Preferences, settings, and ad blocker detection

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide and improve our mapping service
  • Consent: For advertising and analytics (where required)
  • Performance of Contract: To deliver the service you've requested

Cookie and Data Processing Consent

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Please choose your preferences below. Essential cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be disabled.

You can change your preferences at any time by returning to this page. Some features may not work correctly if you disable certain cookies.

Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Local Storage Data: Until you clear your browser data
  • Analytics Data: Up to 26 months
  • Ad Data: As per advertising partner policies

Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Your Choices

You can exercise your rights by:

  • Clearing your browser's local storage and cookies
  • Using browser settings to block cookies
  • Contacting us directly for data requests
  • Using the cookie consent controls on our website

Complaints

If you believe we have not complied with GDPR requirements, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Contact Information

For any GDPR-related queries or to exercise your rights, please contact us through our support channels.
