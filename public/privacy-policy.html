Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our interactive maps, including marker placements, filter preferences, and map navigation patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Local Storage Data: Custom markers, preferences, and settings stored locally on your device.
  • Ad Blocker Detection: We detect the presence of ad blockers to provide alternative monetization options.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our interactive map services
  • Personalizing your experience with saved markers and preferences
  • Displaying relevant advertisements to support our free service
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve map functionality
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse

3. Advertising and Ad Blockers

Our service is supported by advertising revenue. We:

  • Display advertisements from third-party networks
  • Detect ad blocker usage to provide alternative monetization
  • May show popup advertisements to users with ad blockers when using premium features
  • Store ad blocker detection results in hashed form in local storage

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Custom markers and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • We do not store personal identification information on our servers
  • Ad blocker detection data is hashed for privacy protection

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Advertising networks (Google AdSense, etc.)
  • Analytics services to understand user behavior
  • Content delivery networks for faster loading

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to:

  • Remember your map preferences and settings
  • Store custom markers you create
  • Track ad blocker usage for monetization purposes
  • Improve your overall experience

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local storage data at any time
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Request information about data we process
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through our website.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our support channels.
